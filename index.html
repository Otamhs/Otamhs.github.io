Welcome to Critical Forging! On this site you will find our information for placing an order with us or looking into requesting a custom order. all the details can be found throughout the site and contact info can be found below. Please keep in mind that blacksmithing is an involved effort and takes time to complete. Custom orders even more so. Your patience is appreciate while you are waiting on your order.

What is Critical Forging?
Critical Forging is a one man blacksmith forge operated by yours truly.
How long can my order be expected to take?
At this time it is likely to take at least a month, and likely longer. As I am still learning and working towards my ABS (American BladeSmith Society) Journeyman I want to ensure the quality of my product and make sure you are not receiving an inferior product.
How long have you been at this?
At this time only a few months but I am enjoying everything I do and am steadily working towards making more orders and incresing order capacity of what can be filled and shipped in a reasonable amount of time.
